Advent Rangers Drop

P500 Team - 2 chances at 1 chance out of Difficulty Chance

P1000 Team - 3 chances at 1 chance out of Difficulty Chance

P1000 Team at Easy will have less chance than P500 Team at Normal

P1000 Team takes 42 feathers or less for 4 advent rangers at Hard or greater = 2.4 tries
