Advent Rangers Drop Calculator

Assumptions / Options

  • Equal chances given to Leonard Bonus Rewards
  • P500 Team at lower level has less chance than P1 Team at higher level
  • P1000 Team at lower level has less chance than P500 Team at higher level
  • Calculator uses RNG
  • eg. P350, 440 feathers (44 tries), Advent Easy, reward = 7 rangers
    eg. P1000, 2100 feathers (150 tries), Advent Normal, reward = 69 rangers
    eg. P1000, 3519 feathers (207 tries), Advent Hard, reward = 187 rangers
  • eg. P1000, < 42 feathers (2.4 tries), Hard+, reward = 4 rangers
